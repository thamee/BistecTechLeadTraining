# 1:1 Meeting Templates and Notes

## Standard 1:1 Template

### Pre-Meeting Prep
- [ ] Review previous 1:1 notes
- [ ] Check any pending action items
- [ ] Note any observations to discuss
- [ ] Review project progress

### Meeting Structure (30 min)
1. **Opening (2 min)**
   - How are you doing?
   - Anything on your mind?

2. **Their Agenda (15 min)**
   - What would you like to discuss?
   - [Space for their topics]

3. **My Topics (8 min)**
   - [Specific feedback]
   - [Updates they need to know]

4. **Close (5 min)**
   - Action items recap
   - Anything else?
   - Next meeting date

### Post-Meeting
- [ ] Document key points
- [ ] Send action items
- [ ] Schedule any follow-ups

## Customized Templates by Team Member

### Alex (Aspiring Tech Lead)

#### Opening
- Start by asking how Alex feels about his recent progress and workload:  
  - How do you feel about your progress over the last few months?  
  - Any technical challenges or roadblocks?  

#### Key Achievements
- Highlight recent successes and milestones he has achieved  
- Emphasize contributions that had a meaningful impact on the team or projects  
- Recognize leadership potential and mentoring efforts  

#### Next Steps & Growth
- Discuss upcoming responsibilities or initiatives he can lead  
- Suggest certifications or training that would help him move toward a Tech Lead role  
- Assign opportunities to mentor or guide junior engineers (e.g., Casey)  
- Encourage involvement in technical decision-making and stakeholder communication  

#### Sample Questions
- What leadership opportunities would you like to take on next?  
- Are there technical skills you’d like to strengthen?  
- How can I help increase your visibility with stakeholders?  
- Are there any initiatives you want to propose or lead?  

#### Action Items 
Discuss with the management on his initiatives(if any) , 
Revisit his progress in 6months for promotion discussion

### Jordan (Mid-Level – Developing Performance)

#### Opening
- Ask Jordan how he feels about his development and his current projects:  
  - How do you feel about your progress and contributions recently?  
  - Are there aspects of your work you feel confident about, or areas you’d like to improve?  

#### Key Achievements
- Start by recognizing the good work he has done so far  
- Highlight any tasks or projects where he has made measurable contributions  

#### Areas for Improvement
- Discuss areas where performance can improve in the next month  
- Identify key skill gaps or task types he should focus on  
- Discuss timelines for achieving specific goals  
- Encourage reaching out to peers or mentors whenever he encounters challenges  

#### Support & Resources
- Share relevant learning materials, courses, or training opportunities  
- Talk about how management and the team can help him improve and gain confidence  
- Set clear, actionable steps for development  

#### Sample Questions
- What parts of your work are going well, and what areas feel challenging?  
- Where do you feel you need the most support or guidance?  
- Are there specific skills or tasks you’d like to improve over the next month?  
- How can the team or I help you achieve your goals?  

#### Goal of This Review
- Build confidence and clarity around performance expectations  
- Provide actionable guidance to improve skills and task handling  
- Ensure Jordan has the support needed to progress and grow
